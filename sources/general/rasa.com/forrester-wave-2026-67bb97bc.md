## Frequently asked questions

### What is The Forrester Wave™ and why does it matter?

Forrester Wave reports are independent evaluations of technology vendors in a specific market. Forrester analysts score each vendor across 15+ weighted criteria using publicly disclosed data, vendor demonstrations, and customer reference calls. Strong Performer is Forrester’s designation for vendors with meaningful market strength and customer traction.

### Which criteria did Rasa score 5/5 on?

Resource Orchestration & Application Execution, Application Lifecycle Management, and Pricing Flexibility & Transparency. These are the three criteria that most directly determine whether enterprise conversational AI actually runs in production.

### Can Rasa really be deployed fully on-premises?

Yes. Rasa is the only leading conversational AI platform that can be deployed fully on your own infrastructure — including air-gapped environments. You choose where the platform runs, where customer data lives, and which systems it connects to.

### How does Rasa handle data sovereignty and governance?

Customer data never has to leave your environment. Rasa supports version control, audit logging, role-based access control, and change approval workflows — the enterprise governance controls your compliance and security teams expect. See the ‘Why Rasa’ section above for more detail.

### Who uses Rasa in regulated industries?

Rasa is trusted by Fortune 500 companies in banking, telecom, healthcare, insurance, and retail — including Deutsche Telekom, Swisscom, Autodesk, Providence Health, Albert Heijn, N26, and Groupe IMA.
