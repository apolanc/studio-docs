## Why partner with us

Move Beyond POCs

Partners get the expertise they need to move customers from POC to production deployments seamlessly at scale.

Recurring Revenue Streams

Every engagement opens the door to strategy, architecture, implementation and managed services, and expansion, all sourced from Rasa-generated leads.

Built for Regulated Industries

Rasa is designed for markets where conversational AI has the highest stakes and the highest barriers to entry.

Expand Across Use Cases

Our multi-agent architecture means a successful deployment expands horizontally. A first engagement typically expands to 3-5 business units within 12 months.

## Who we partner with

System Integrator

Provides services from proof of concept to production deployment, including implementation, consultancy, and IT domain expertise (including professional services and staff augmentation).

Consulting

Provides strategy consulting to advise Customers on a range of relevant topics like Customer Experience, AI, Digital Transformation, and Conversation Design.

Referral

Provides a mutual referral partnership where Rasa directs customers to our Partner for their implementation and consulting services, and the Partner refers customers to Rasa for our software license.

OEM

Guide buyers through complex purchase flows with fewer drop-offs and more completed checkouts.

Technology

Let prospects explore your product or service through conversational flows that show value quickly.

Reseller

Turn static marketing pages into interactive experiences that improve engagement and time on site.

## Partner prerequisites

✓

Technical Foundation

Demonstrable conversational AI experience and Python-based application development.

✓

Vertical Market Expertise

Active practice, relationships, and/or client base in at least one of Rasa's key sectors: banking, financial services, telco, healthcare, retail, airlines, government

✓

Partnership Intent

Willingness to co-sell, register deals, and participate in at least one go-to-market activity per quarter.

✓

Team Commitment

A named team of three or more engineers willing to complete [**Rasa University**](https://rasa.com/university) within 90 days of joining.

## Steps to being a Rasa Partner

1

#### Apply

Fill out the [**partner form**](https://rasa.com/partners#join). There's no cost or commitment. The Rasa team will review and respond within 5 days.

2

#### Foundations

Complete the [**Rasa University**](https://rasa.com/university) Foundations track. Estimated investment 3–4 hours per developer.

3

#### Unlock the Hub

Get access to exclusive content: sales playbooks, technical deep dives, certification, deal registration.

4

#### Certification

Technical Engineers and Sales Team complete certification through a mix of in-person and online courses.

5

#### Demo Capability

Finalize your vertical and/or solution-relevant demo to build prospect confidence.

6

#### Practice Growth

Dedicated partner success support, referrals, and joint GTM effort continuously scale your benefits as a Rasa partner.
