## Perks and Benefits

Flexible work schedule

Flexible schedules, remote opportunities, and the ability to work from home creates a balance between work and daily life. We truly unplug during time off and highly encourage employees to take at least two consecutive weeks of paid time off per year.

Opportunities for growth

We encourage you to grow, learn, and push yourself. We empower employees to take ownership of their work and provide a yearly budget for professional development.

Stock Options

We believe in our mission and in winning together. We invest in employees' futures and ensure everyone has a stake in the company.

Team offsite

We meet annually in an exciting European city to plan and set company goals. As a distributed company, this is also our chance to have fun and get to know more about each other in a face-to-face setting.

## Our Values

Our values define how we work and are at the heart of our culture. This behavior inspires us to achieve our mission and vision as a team. We thrive because we integrate these principles into our everyday processes, from interviews to onboarding, feedback, and praise.

### We Act as One Team

Everything is interconnected: we win together. Our team works as one, and every team member is crucial to our success.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/68e69c204ba0666edacc94b1/68e93a89c1ea8454b2ed2017_gradient%20spotlight.avif)

### We Are Empowered to Take Action

Every day matters. We take initiative, move with urgency, and finish what we start to accelerate results.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/68e69c204ba0666edacc94b1/68e93a89c1ea8454b2ed2017_gradient%20spotlight.avif)

### We Embrace Integrity & Diversity

Our team is inclusive and diverse. We admit to our mistakes, and with honesty and integrity, create a space that addresses challenges with empathy.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/68e69c204ba0666edacc94b1/68e93a89c1ea8454b2ed2017_gradient%20spotlight.avif)

### We Own Customer Outcomes

We hold ourselves accountable for helping our customers deliver conversational AI experiences their customers love.
