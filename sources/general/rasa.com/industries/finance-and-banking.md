## Built for high-value, high-stakes conversations

### Customer Account Support & Self-Service

Handle high-volume requests like balance checks, card activations, address updates, and password resets with AI agents connected directly to banking systems. Reduce contact center load while maintaining accuracy and control.

### Secure Transactions & Payments

Guide customers through payments, transfers, and transaction inquiries with structured verification, confirmation steps, and policy-controlled execution. Sensitive actions are handled safely, with clear logic governing every step.

### Authentication & Fraud-related workflows

Guide customers through identity verification and fraud scenarios, adapting the conversation based on risk signals and escalating when needed, with context carried across every step.

### Proactive Service and Customer Engagement

Send reminders, alerts, and next-best actions based on customer context and business rules, making outreach timely and relevant instead of generic.

### Knowledge Search & Answer Resolution

Answer questions about fees, policies, product terms, and service processes using trusted internal knowledge, so customers get consistent and reliable information.

### Loan & Credit Application Support

Support eligibility checks, disclosures, document collection, and applications for loans and credit products, keeping the experience continuous from start to submission.

## Frequently asked questions

### What types of customer interactions can Rasa automate in banking?

Rasa enables banks to automate high-volume and high-stakes interactions such as account servicing, payments, authentication, fraud handling, and lending workflows. These are not just responses, but complete, policy-compliant actions executed across backend systems.

### How is Rasa different from traditional banking chatbots or AI assistants?

Traditional chatbots focus on answering questions. Rasa enables AI agents that take action across systems, using orchestrated capabilities that follow business rules, maintain context, and execute real workflows end to end.

### Is Rasa compliant for financial services use cases?

Yes. Rasa supports SOC 2 Type II compliance, on-premise and private cloud deployment, data residency controls, and full audit logging for every automated interaction. This makes it suitable for environments with strict regulatory and compliance requirements.

### Can Rasa handle voice interactions for banking?

Yes. Rasa provides a single agent system across voice, chat, mobile apps, and web. Context is maintained across channels, so users can switch touchpoints without restarting their interaction.

### How does Rasa integrate with banking systems?

Rasa connects to core banking platforms, CRMs, payment processors, authentication services, and fraud detection systems through open APIs and pre-built connectors. Teams use Python logic and custom connectors to integrate with proprietary tools without vendor lock-in.

### How does Rasa ensure safe and compliant AI behavior?

Rasa separates language understanding from execution. AI models interpret user input, but all actions are governed by structured, versioned logic that enforces policies, validation steps, and approval paths. Every decision is traceable and auditable.

### How much control do teams have over AI models and system behavior?

Rasa gives teams full control over model selection, prompts, orchestration logic, and execution paths. Every part of the system is configurable and observable, so teams can adapt behavior without relying on vendor-controlled black boxes.

### What financial institutions use Rasa?

Enterprise financial institutions including N26, ERGO Group, nib Group, PayPal, and Bank of America use Rasa to automate customer interactions across account servicing, transactions, fraud support, and engagement. Rasa powers production deployments handling millions of interactions.

### How is Rasa different from other conversational AI platforms for banking?

Rasa is built for organizations that need control, extensibility, and long-term ownership. Instead of limiting customization or hiding logic behind vendor systems, Rasa provides a platform that teams can operate, extend, and govern as part of their core infrastructure.

### How does Rasa support fraud detection and authentication?

Rasa supports structured authentication and fraud handling by integrating with existing security systems. It can enforce identity checks, trigger step-up authentication, and route sensitive interactions through governed workflows without relying on model-generated decisions.
