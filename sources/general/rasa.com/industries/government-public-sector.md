Every interaction is structured, traceable, and aligned with agency policy, enabling digital service delivery without sacrificing accountability. 

Public-sector organizations, including the Portugal Tax Authority, use Rasa to modernize citizen services while maintaining full control over infrastructure, data, and regulatory requirements.

## Built for high-value, high-stakes conversations

### High-volume Citizen Service Automation

Resolve common requests such as address changes, document reissuance, and status inquiries across channels, reducing contact center pressure while maintaining service quality.

### Program Enrollment and Benefits Processing

Guide citizens through eligibility checks, applications, renewals, and document submission with workflows aligned to program rules, improving completion rates and reducing processing delays.

### Permitting, Licensing & Regulatory Workflows

Execute permitting, licensing, and other rule-based processes with validation and structured intake, ensuring submissions meet regulatory requirements.

### Proactive Communication and Service Continuity

Deliver timely, contextual updates and enable two-way interaction for service follow-ups and issue reporting.

### Identity verification and secure interactions

Enable access to sensitive services by integrating identity checks and authentication into service workflows without disrupting the user experience.

### Multilingual & Accessible Service Delivery

Provide multilingual and accessible interactions across channels, supporting equitable access to public services.

## Frequently asked questions

### What public-sector services can Rasa automate?

Rasa can automate citizen services such as case status inquiries, benefits workflows, permit applications, document requests, scheduling, and public program support.

### Can Rasa be deployed in regulated public-sector environments?

Yes. Rasa supports deployment models that keep infrastructure and data within agency-controlled environments.

### Can Rasa support voice and digital channels together?

Yes. Rasa enables one coordinated service experience across web, voice, mobile, kiosk, and IVR.

### How does Rasa integrate with government systems?

Rasa connects to case management systems, benefits platforms, permitting databases, CRMs, and document management systems through APIs, custom actions, and MCP connectors.

### What government organizations use Rasa?

Public-sector organizations including the Portugal Tax Authority use Rasa to automate citizen interactions. Portugal’s national tax agency selected Rasa to handle citizen tax queries at scale while maintaining on-prem deployment, full data control, and EU regulatory compliance.

### How is Rasa different from a typical government chatbot?

Rasa supports AI agents that execute workflows across systems, not just bots that answer questions or redirect users to forms and web pages.

### Can Rasa support multilingual government services?

Yes. Rasa supports multilingual interactions and accessible service delivery across channels, helping agencies serve diverse populations more effectively.

### How does Rasa handle policy changes in government workflows?

Rasa makes it easier for agencies to adapt services when policies change. Teams can update workflow logic, eligibility criteria, required steps, and service rules in a controlled way, then test changes before rollout.
